03 About

A builder at the seam
of code & thought.

Hi — I'm AM-SuSh. I build AI agents and research tools, and I'm quietly obsessed with one question: where does machine intelligence meet human understanding? My work lives between systems that are rigorous and ideas worth chasing.

你好。我造 AI 智能体与研究工具，也痴迷于同一个问题—— 机器的智能，究竟在何处与人的理解相接。我相信好的工程是克制的， 好的研究是诚实的；它们都始于好奇，终于一行能让人安心的代码。
